Introduction To Computing

Introduction to Computing is a foundational subject that provides

students with a broad overview of the fundamental concepts and

principles related to computing. The course is designed to introduce

individuals to the world of computers, information technology, and the

basics of computer science. Computer basics, computer software,

and information representation are key components typically

covered in an Introduction to Computing subject
